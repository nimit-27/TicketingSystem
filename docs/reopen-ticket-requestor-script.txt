Script: Reopening a Ticket (Requestor)

Steps
1. Open **My Tickets** (or **All Tickets**) from the menu.
2. Find the ticket with status **Resolved/Closed** and open it.
3. Click **Reopen**.
4. Enter the reason for reopening (include the issue still observed).
5. Click **Submit/Confirm** to reopen.
6. Verify the status changes to **Reopened** and your comment is visible.

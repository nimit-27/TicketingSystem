Script: RNO Severity Recommendation

Steps
1. Open **Tickets Assigned to Me** from the dashboard.
2. Select the ticket that needs a severity recommendation.
3. Review the subject, description, category, and any attachments.
4. Click **Recommend Severity**.
5. Choose the recommended severity level and add a brief justification.
6. Click **Submit** to save the recommendation.
7. Confirm the ticket shows your recommended severity and notes.
